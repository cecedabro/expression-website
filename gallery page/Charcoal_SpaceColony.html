A charcoal drawing perspective exercise! - With a bit of added imagination

This was an exercise to practice perspective.

The teacher had arranged a couple of items, each with unique but recognisable shapes.

It was mainly meant to practice with perspective lines, as you might be able to tell from the slightly blurred lines.

After drawing the shapes I let my imagination run wild and decided to turn it into a martian colony!

You can see a planet (or moon) get destroyed above the horizon!

(I also turned it into something else to have fun with a girl at that writing course)
