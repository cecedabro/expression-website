A small silent life I gave to my grandparents

This was a small silent life I made as birthdaypresent for my French grandparents!

Something I struggled with was the shadows and perspective. I didn't really think about where my light source was coming from and kinda just "improvised" shadows.

I'm happy with the way it turned out though! It's a great exercise for colour mixing, shading and abstract shapes understanding.
