Welcome to my first ever oil painting

    This is was my first ever oil painting! 
    I learnt a very important lesson with this one...
        
    When its finished, its finished! 
    Don't overcomplicate whats done. 
        
    Here is the painting how it looked after I kept 
    working on it even though it was basically done...
    
    I kept adding more and more. 
    I thought that by blending the colours,
    adding texture that it would turn out 
    "better". 
    
    But to be honest I think the piece was finished 
    when it looked like the previous image.
