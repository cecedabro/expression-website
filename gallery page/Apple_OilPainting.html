Welcome to my first ever oil painting

This was my first ever oil painting!

I learnt a very important lesson with this one...

When it's finished, it's finished! Don't overcomplicate what's done.

Here is the painting how it looked after I kept working on it even though it was basically done...

I kept adding more and more. I thought that by blending the colours, adding texture that it would turn out "better".

But to be honest I think the piece was finished when it looked like the previous image.
